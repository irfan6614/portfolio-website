My Story

From Curiosity to Craft

I'm Muhammad Irfan — a final-year Computer Science student at the University of Agriculture Faisalabad, where I've maintained a rank in the top 5% of my batch. My journey into data science began with a simple question: why do patterns exist in data, and how can machines learn to see them?

That curiosity led me to build fraud detection systems, stock volatility models, and NLP pipelines — not as exercises, but as functional, deployable software. My internships at SkillifyZone, MAHEEN Textile, and Punjab Police gave me hands-on experience across data analytics, IT infrastructure, and enterprise networking.

I believe great technology is invisible — it just works. Whether that means a well-tuned Random Forest, a clean FastAPI endpoint, or a Power BI dashboard a business actually uses, I focus on outcomes that matter.
